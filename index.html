What is Map Camp? It started as a tweet from Simon Wardley asking if anyone would like to have "a small meetup in London on mapping". It had so much interest that it quickly turned into a full day event. Map Camp is a one day London based conference including workshops and talks about mapping techniques used in organisations.

General Tickets are no longer available. Add your name at the bottom of the volunteer spreadsheet to join the waiting list.

Diversity Tickets have been reserved and are still available. People in underrepresented groups are encouraged to apply. To reduce the barrier to attendance, a limited number of bursaries are also available to assist those with costs related to caring responsibilities, such as childcare.
